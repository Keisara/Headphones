Audio-Technica ATH-M50 Studio Monitor Headphones

$119.99

Pros:

  • Good bass, Decent Isolation, Great Value, Comfortable, Not Fatiguing, Neutral
  • Pristine sound quality, Great build, Price, Versatile
Cons:
  • Pleather pads (for some), Perhaps too much bass
  • Heavy clamp, but you'll get use to it

Sennheiser HD 558

$104.99

Pros:

  • Forward, engaging mids. Overall euphonic, non-fatiguing, super comfortable, etc
  • Pristine sound clarity for it's price. Very neutral sound having the right amount of everything. Very very comfortable can be worn for hours.
Cons:
  • Lacking deep bass extension
  • Mid-bass bleed, slightly veiled

AKG K550

$170.90

Pros:

  • Open Stage, No Congestion at High Volumes, Quality Built, Reference Response Curve, Comfort
  • Looks, Build Quality, Decent tonality and fairly flat FR from midrange down to bass, no treble emphasis, decent soundstage for a closed headphone
Cons:
  • Artificial Treble (can be improved with burn in & DAC/amplifier), Earcups Maybe Too Large For Some
  • Serious peak and resonance in one spot in the upper mids ruins an otherwise decent headphone

Sennheiser HD 650 Headphones

$499.95

Pros:

  • Smooth Presentation, a linear, stout sound at louder volumes.
  • Sensational Excursion Factor , Exquisite Bass Extension , Almost perfect Mid-Range , Classic HE-60/90 Design , Potential Upper Treble Extension
Cons:
  • Built quality & comfort have room for improvement
  • Slightly soft sounding, some grain in the treble, bass rolls off early in the sub bass, clamp, ear cups aren't deep enough

Beyerdynamic DT990 Premium 250 Ohm

$239.00

Pros:

  • Soundstage - amazing. Good bass, great separation of instruments, and beautiful albeit recessed mids
  • Amazing comfort, revealing detailed sound
Cons:
  • Trebles, build could be better
  • Can be slightly fatiguing to some on the high end if not properly amped.

V-MODA Crossfade M-100

$240.00

Pros:

  • Excellent mobile headphone, really great chest-hitting bass, fairly detailed, non-fatiguing treble, folding mechanism is sturdy, modular design
  • Crisp and clear highs, punchy bass.
Cons:
  • Shallow earpads, odd presentation of instruments/soundstage, too much bass, laid-back midrange, laid-back upper-treble, fragile zipper on case
  • Hot, low mids, uncomfortable.
